Counseling & Student Development Center
The Counseling & Student Development Center offers support to the UH Manoa Students to assist with mental health, personal, or academic conversation.

Business Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:30 am - 4:30 pm HST
Location: Queen Lili'uokalani Center for Student Services, Room 312

http://www.manoa.hawaii.edu/counseling/

Kokua Program

The Kokua program is part of the UH MƒÅnoa office for students with disabilities. They serve undergraduate, graduate, and professional students with learning, physical, psychiatric, and other document disabilities. They work to facilitate equal access for students with disabilities to the Manoa experience.

https://www.hawaii.edu/kokua/

Women's Center

The Women's Center provides personal and academic support of the success of women and LGBT students at UHM. Due to COVID-19 the physical office is temporarily closed. However, the staff can be reached via email (uhmwomen@hawaii.edu) or by phone to schedule an appointment. All appointments will be via phone or zoom.

Business Hours: TBA
Location: Queen Lili'uokalani Center for Student Services, Room 211
Phone: (808) 956-8059

https://www.hawaii.edu/womenscenter/

University Health Services

The University Health Services Manoa (UHSM) is a unit within the Division of Student Success under the Vice Provost for Student Success. It is staffed by physicians, nurse practitioners, nurses, nutritionists, pharmacists, laboratory technicians, and other support staff. A wide range of medical services and programs are offered to students, faculty, and staff.

Business Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 am - 4:00 pm
Location: Behind Kennedy Theater on Upper Campus of UHM
Phone: (808) 956-8965

http://www.hawaii.edu/shs/

Office of Title IX
The office of Title IX is a unit of federal civil rights has the specific responsibility for providing prompt and effective responses to all complains of sex discrimination or harassment for faculty, staff, and students. The UHM community, guests, and visitors have the right to be free from all forms of sex/gender harassment, discrimination and misconduct, examples of which can include any acts of sexual violence, sexual harassment, domestic violence, dating violence and stalking.

https://manoa.hawaii.edu/titleix/
